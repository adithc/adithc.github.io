Meet the People Behind the Product

Nishant Malpani (CO-CEO)

Nishant is a rising senior at Redmond High School in Redmond, WA. He has been playing premier soccer for his whole life, and loves the competetive yet fun atmosphere that it brings. As a younger kid, he loved playing sports with older kids and still cherishes the memories he had with them. He hopes to bring these memories to as many children as possible!

Savith Vijendra (CO-CEO)

Savith is a rising senior at Ottawa Hills High School. Savith fell in love with tennis at a young age and has been playing consistently ever since. The memories that Savith has made at tournaments and at team practices are some of the best memories that he can remember. Wanting to provide this joy to everyone, Savith hopes to use Ludis as a platform to help kids follow their passions and love out their sports dreams.
